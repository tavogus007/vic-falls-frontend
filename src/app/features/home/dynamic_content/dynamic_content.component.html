About Victoria Falls Regional Institute

Victoria Falls Research Institute is a pan African independent academic and research institute with operational bases in Zimbabwe and Kenya.

We seek to localize the power of knowledge in the fields of, International Law, International Relations, Peace & Security, Governance, Development and Humanitarian Affairs

Why We Exist?

Africa is not only rich in human and natural resources. It possesses wealth, wisdom and knowledge. However, its wisdom and knowledge are often demeaned, unrecognised and marginalised; including by some Africans, but more importantly within the global knowledge systems.

A common trend that limits African knowledge and wisdom's role in shaping global research agendas and policies is distance. Discussions, Policy Formulations and Conferences about Africa tend to be held without Africa. They are held outside Africa. Those who possess African wisdom, knowledge and experiences are often denied or have limited access to such platforms, due to many factors including visa and financial limitations.

Vision

To inspire African Rennaissance.

Mission

To bridge the gap between local knowledge and global research and policy- making; facilitate dialogue towards the creation of African narratives through extensive knowledge production and dissemination.
