About Victoria Falls Regional Institute

Victoria Falls Research Institute is a pan African independent academic and research institute with operational bases in Zimbabwe and Kenya.

We seek to localize the power of knowledge in the fields of, International Law, International Relations, Peace & Security, Governance, Development and Humanitarian Affairs

Why We Exist?

Africa is not only rich in human and natural resources. It possesses wealth, wisdom and knowledge. However, its wisdom and knowledge are often demeaned, unrecognised and marginalised; including by some Africans, but more importantly within the global knowledge systems.

A common trend that limits African knowledge and wisdom's role in shaping global research agendas and policies is distance. Discussions, Policy Formulations and Conferences about Africa tend to be held without Africa. They are held outside Africa. Those who possess African wisdom, knowledge and experiences are often denied or have limited access to such platforms, due to many factors including visa and financial limitations.

Vision

To inspire African Rennaissance.

Mission

To bridge the gap between local knowledge and global research and policy- making; facilitate dialogue towards the creation of African narratives through extensive knowledge production and dissemination.

Dr. James Nyawo

Founding Executive Director

James Nyawo holds a Ph.D. in International Law from Middlesex University, UK, and is a Zimbabwean scholar-practitioner with over 20 years of experience in humanitarian field management and academia. His fieldwork includes assignments in Angola, Northern Uganda, Sudan, and South Sudan. He has consulted for UN agencies like UNICEF, IOM, and UNESCO. Nyawo specializes in International Law, International Relations, Security, and Terrorism and is a lecturer at Strathmore University in Nairobi, Kenya, where he teaches related subjects. He has lectured internationally and published extensively on Africa and international criminal justice, particularly regarding the enforcement of International Criminal Law by the ICC in Africa. Nyawo is also involved in organizations such as ICMICA-MIIC Pax Romana Africa and the Buhera Development Forum. He is deeply committed to the Pax Africana philosophy and the revitalization of Africa.

Dr. Saul Davis Sango

Director

Dr. Saul Davis Sango holds a B.Sc. in Statistics and Data Analysis and an M.Sc. in Industrial Diversity & Organizational Psychology, and is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Psychology. He is a Kenyan training consultant and business development strategist with extensive experience across Africa, working with institutions such as Empowerment Resource Technologies, World Governance Centre, Devrell International, and the Victoria Falls Regional Institute for Strategic Studies. Dr. Sango is a Six Sigma Black Belt (SSBB) and PMP-certified professional, specializing in organizational psychology, data and insights analysis, and change management. He is passionate about performance enhancement, leadership development, emotional intelligence, and digital transformation. An author and trusted advisor, he is also affiliated with organizations in research, development, and strategic consulting, and is a strong advocate for data-driven decision-making and organizational excellence in the African context.
